## Hi there, I'm Muhammad Tanveer 👋

**Senior Staff Android Engineer · Fintech · POS · Mobile Payments**

I build secure, production-grade Android applications and lead engineering teams. Over 6+ years I've specialized in payment systems — multi-module POS platforms, payment-terminal/hardware integrations, and the architecture that keeps financial transactions reliable at scale.

📍 Karachi, Pakistan · 🟢 **Open to senior / staff Android roles** (remote & relocation)

---

### 🛠️ What I work on

- **Production Android architecture** — Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, Clean Architecture, MVVM/MVI, Coroutines & Flow, Hilt DI
- **Payments & POS** — multi-module POS platforms for U.S. retail & QSR, payment-terminal SDK integration, transaction reliability
- **Engineering leadership** — leading teams of 5–7, code quality, testing strategy, mentorship
- **Currently exploring** — AI in Android and research-centric engineering, alongside graduate study in Europe

---

### 💼 Selected experience

- Led Android engineering on a **multi-module POS platform** serving U.S. retail & QSR operations, from architecture through delivery.
- Integrated **payment-terminal hardware SDKs** (card-present flows, EMV, certification) for in-store transaction processing.
- Shipped consumer & fintech apps reaching **3M+ users**, with a focus on security, offline-first reliability, and clean architecture.

> Most of my recent work is proprietary. Happy to walk through architecture decisions, trade-offs, and impact in conversation.
